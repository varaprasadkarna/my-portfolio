Overview

Non-invasive blood group detection is a method of identifying an individual’s blood type (A, B, AB, or O with Rh factor) without collecting blood samples using spectral images. Unlike the traditional method which is invasive, painful, and more prone to human errors, this project presents a modern, painless alternative. By leveraging spectral imaging and advanced machine learning algorithms, it analyzes subtle differences in light absorption and reflection from the skin to accurately predict the blood group. This innovative approach has the potential to revolutionize medical diagnostics, making blood typing faster, safer, and more accessible for everyone.
