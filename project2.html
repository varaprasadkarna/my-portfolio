GeneCorrector.ai

Pipelining Machine Learning and Generative AI for advanced genetic insights.

Highlights

Overview

This project introduces an innovative dual-pipeline approach to genomic analysis. By first leveraging a highly accurate Machine Learning model, we can effectively detect and classify the status of specific genes. Following this, a powerful Generative AI model is used to perform gene reconstruction. This combination allows for not only precise identification of genetic markers but also the ability to generate realistic, synthetic gene sequences, which is invaluable for research, drug discovery, and understanding complex genetic disorders. The synergy between predictive ML and creative GenAI marks a significant step forward in computational biology.
